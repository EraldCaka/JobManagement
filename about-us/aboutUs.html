About Us

This website was created in 2022 by a group of students from the University of New York Tirana (UNYT). The main goal of this company is to manage the job market in Albania. We are a team of 2 people, who have a lot of experience in the field of web development and we are ready to help you find the job you are looking for. We can help you manage your employees wages and salaries, and we can also help you set up schedules for your employees. We are also ready to help you manage your company's finances. We are here to help you manage your company in the best way possible and we are ready to help you grow your business.
